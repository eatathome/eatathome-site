2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Eat at Home:

Account information

  • Email address and name (if you create an account)
  • Device identifiers and push notification tokens

Financial data (via Plaid)

  • Bank account and transaction data accessed through Plaid Technologies, Inc.
  • This includes transaction amounts, merchant names, and transaction dates
  • We access this data in read-only mode — we cannot move, transfer, or modify your funds
  • We use this data solely to identify restaurant visits and grocery purchases for savings calculations

Usage data

  • Meals logged, streaks, and in-app activity
  • App crash reports and performance data
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Calculate and display your home cooking savings
  • Detect restaurant and grocery transactions automatically
  • Maintain your streak, milestones, and savings history
  • Send you notifications about your savings (with your permission)
  • Improve the app and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your financial data for advertising, credit decisions, or any purpose unrelated to the core savings-tracking features of the app.

4. Plaid and financial data

Eat at Home uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. to securely connect to your financial accounts. When you link your accounts through Plaid:

  • Your banking credentials are entered directly into Plaid's secure interface — we never see your username or password
  • Plaid retrieves your transaction data and shares it with us in read-only form
  • Your use of Plaid is also subject to Plaid's Privacy Policy and Plaid's End User Privacy Policy
  • You can disconnect your accounts at any time from within the app

We access your financial data only to calculate savings. We never sell, rent, or share your financial data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use third-party services including Plaid (financial data), Google AdMob (advertising), and crash analytics tools. Each is contractually bound to use your data only as directed by us.
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may transfer to the new entity under the same privacy commitments.

6. Advertising

Eat at Home may display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) to show relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings under Privacy > Tracking. We do not share your financial or savings data with AdMob.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Financial data accessed through Plaid is subject to Plaid's security infrastructure, which meets bank-level security standards. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's privacy

Eat at Home is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your financial accounts at any time from within the app
  • Opt out of push notifications in your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@eatathome.app.

11. California residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information). To make a request, contact us at privacy@eatathome.app.
