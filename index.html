How it works

Three steps to seeing
your savings grow

01

Connect your accounts

Securely link your bank with Plaid. Eat at Home detects restaurant visits and grocery trips automatically with read-only, bank-level encryption.

02

Log your home meals

Tap once when you cook dinner. The app estimates your meal cost based on typical grocery costs in your area along with your actual grocery spending, and calculates what you saved vs. dining out.

03

Watch the number grow

See your cumulative savings build up over time. Hit milestones. Keep your streak going. The money you were already saving finally has a scoreboard.

Features

Built for people who need a little
encouragement to stay in for dinner

📊

Automatic savings tracking

Your baseline is your actual restaurant spend — so savings are calculated against what you really would have spent, not some average.

🔗

Plaid-powered detection

Restaurant visits are detected automatically. No manual entry, no spreadsheets. Just cook and check in.

🔥

Streaks & milestones

Build a home-cooking habit with streaks and savings tiers. Positive reinforcement, not guilt.

📈

Monthly savings reports

A clear breakdown of what you saved each month — meals logged, money saved, and how you're trending over time.

🛡️

Read-only & secure

Eat at Home never touches your money. Plaid connects read-only so your accounts stay fully in your control.

🍽️

Dinner focused

One meal a day, tracked simply. No complex logging, no calorie counting — just whether you cooked or went out.
